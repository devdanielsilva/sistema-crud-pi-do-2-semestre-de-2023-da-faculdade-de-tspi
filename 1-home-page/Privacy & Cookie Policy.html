Privacy and security policy

Alpha Games e Informática is committed to transparency, privacy and security of its customers' data throughout the entire interaction process with our website and physical store.

So that there are no doubts regarding what information we collect and how we use, store or delete it, we detail our Privacy Policy below.

Customer registration data is not disclosed to third parties, except when necessary for the delivery process for billing or participation in promotions requested by customers. Your personal data is essential for the order to arrive safely at your home, within the stipulated delivery time.

To help you understand our Privacy Policy, we present a summary:

Data processing agent: Alpha Games.

Alpha Games' role in data processing: Predominantly controller.

Type of personal information collected: Alpha Games collects information that the Customer provides when registering and creating an account in “My Account”, on our website or when making a purchase or exchanging products in our physical store.

For what purpose do we use your personal data?

  • • To deliver purchased products or services;
  • • To keep you informed about the status of your order;
  • • To coordinate with partners the delivery or availability of services;
  • • To carry out exchanges;
  • • To send communication about services and provide a better customer experience, making Alpha Games even more relevant in your daily life.

Your rights

The General Data Protection Law (LGPD) gives the customer the right to request Nova Era Games e Informática, at any time, through a formal request, information regarding the data processed.
